CSChE 2025 — Canadian Chemical Engineering Conference

Dates: October 5–7, 2025 · Location: Palais des Congrès de Montréal, QC

About the Conference
  • Flagship event for chemical engineers in Canada, drawing 1,100+ delegates from around the world.
  • Meaningful exchange through symposia, workshops, poster sessions, and keynote talks.
  • Explore new ideas, recognize achievements, and connect with the community.
What to Expect
  • Technical symposia across core and emerging areas of chemical engineering.
  • Hands-on workshops and networking with industry and academia.
  • Student poster sessions and community events.

CSChE UofT Delegation — Funding & Logistics

How This Works
  • This form collects interest only; it is not official registration.

Planned Support (if approved)
  • Partial (≈40–60%) reimbursement for transportation (train/bus), lodging (hotel), and delegate registration.

Why Join
  • Travel with peers to Montreal for the national conference and build your network.
  • The department aims to avoid midterms/major deadlines during the conference dates.
Costs & Subsidy Notes
  • Reference: CSChE 2023 trip cost ≈ $320 per delegate after subsidy for ~30 students (full cost often $900+ without subsidy).
  • Eligibility: to qualify for subsidy, complete conference registration during the early-bird period.
